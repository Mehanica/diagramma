12 min

serves 2

Spaghetti

gorgonzola and grapes

Ingredients

  • SPAGHETTI MAKFA bronze die 160 g
  • Double cream 200 ml
  • Gorgonzola (or another blue cheese) 40 g
  • Seedless grapes 100 g
  • Mint 5 g
  • Basil leaves 5 g
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper

Cooking

  1. Boil spaghetti al dente.
  2. Cut grapes in half, place them in a pan and toast.
  3. Heat the cream with gorgonzola cheese until the cheese completely dissolve, add toasted grapes and stir for 2-3 min.
  4. Once the spaghetti is ready, add the drained pasta into the pan and stir with sauce for 1 min.
  5. Finally, add some chopped mint, a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Serve on a plate with basil garnish.
