Campanelle

with mussels, olives, parmigiana and passata

Ingredients

  • Campanelle MAKFA bronze die 120 g
  • Olive oil 30 ml
  • Mussels 300 g
  • Olives 200 g
  • Passata 240 g
  • Parmesan 20 g
  • Garlic 5 g
  • Basil leaves 10 g

Cooking

  1. Boil Campanelle al dente.
  2. Clean mussels and fry them with olive oil and chopped garlic for 1,5-2 min.
  3. Add passata, olives and stew at low heat for a further 2-3 min.
  4. Once Campanelle is ready, place it in the pan with mussels, add grated parmesan, a pinch of salt, pepper and mix.
  5. Sprinkle with grated parmesan over the pasta and garnish with basil to serve.

Spaghetti

gorgonzola and grapes

Ingredients

  • SPAGHETTI MAKFA bronze die 160 g
  • Double cream 200 ml
  • Gorgonzola (or another blue cheese) 40 g
  • Seedless grapes 100 g
  • Mint 5 g
  • Basil leaves 5 g
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper

Cooking

  1. Boil spaghetti al dente.
  2. Cut grapes in half, place them in a pan and toast.
  3. Heat the cream with gorgonzola cheese until the cheese completely dissolve, add toasted grapes and stir for 2-3 min.
  4. Once the spaghetti is ready, add the drained pasta into the pan and stir with sauce for 1 min.
  5. Finally, add some chopped mint, a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Serve on a plate with basil garnish.

Fusilli

with chili pepper, veal and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fusilli MAKFA bronze die 250 g
  • Chili pepper (1/3 of pod) 5 g
  • Veal 400 g
  • Olive oil 60 ml
  • Coriander leaves 50 g
  • Tin diced tomatoes 400 g
  • Parmesan 50 g

Cooking

  1. Boil Fusilli al dente.
  2. Add fine chopped chili pepper without seeds, coriander leaves, tin diced tomatoes and stew at low heat for 1,5 -2 min.
  3. Place Fusili in the pan with sauce and stir.
  4. Add grated parmesan, salt and pepper.
  5. Sprinkle with grated parmesan to serve and garnish with coriander leaves.

Farfalle

with mussels and mint

Ingredients

  • Farfalle MAKFA bronze die 260 g
  • White wine 70 ml
  • Almonds 100 g
  • Olive oil 60 ml
  • Mint 10 g
  • Double cream 140 ml
  • Parmesan 20 g
  • Seasoning:
  • Dried herbs 5 g
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper

Cooking

  1. Boil Farfalle al dente.
  2. Chopped almonds and toast them.
  3. Cook half of almonds with white wine for 2-3 min, add double cream and stew a bit more until it becomes a sauce.
  4. Place Farfalle, dried herbs in the pan with the sauce, mix and add a half of parmesan, chopped mint, salt and pepper.
  5. Scatter with the rest of toasted almonds, grated parmesan and garnish with mint to serve.

Penne Rigate

with fried rocket, prawns and double cream

Ingredients

  • Penne Rigate MAKFA bronze die 250 g
  • Rocket 60 g
  • Tiger prawns 300 g
  • Olive oil 60 ml
  • Double cream 400 ml
  • Parmesan 50 g

Cooking

  1. Boil Penne Rigate al dente.
  2. Clean tiger prawns and fry them with olive oil for 1,5-2 min.
  3. Once cooked, add rocket, double cream and stew at low heat for a further 1,5-2 min.
  4. Place cooked Penne Rigate in the pan with the sauce, stir while it is stewing for a min.
  5. At the end, add grated parmesan, salt and pepper, food is ready to serve.

Cavatappi

with chanterelles mushrooms and blue cheese cream sauce

Ingredients

  • Cavatappi MAKFA bronze die 250 g
  • Chanterelles mushrooms 150 g
  • Double cream 200 g
  • Blue cheese 50 g
  • Parmesan 30 g
  • Parsley 15 g
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper

Cooking

  1. Boil Cavatappi al dente.
  2. Stew mushrooms with double cream for 3-4 min, add blue cheese, stir until the cheese completely dissolve.
  3. Once Cavatappi cooked, place it in the pan with sauce, add salt, pepper, a half of grated parmesan, and chopped parsley, mix.
  4. Sprinkle with the rest of grated parmesan over the pasta to serve.
